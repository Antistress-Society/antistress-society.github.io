Antistress Society

Welcome to the official website of the Antistress Society association!

Mission

Antistress Society is a non-profit, secular association that does not pursue any profit, but works exclusively for the common good. It coordinates its activities at home and abroad.

The primary goal of Antistress Society is to instantly free all individuals from stress as well as to provide everyone with a long-term stress relief. Antistress Society does not differentiate whatsoever between age, gender, race, belief, worldview, culture and social background. It achieves its goals by teaching universally applicable and time-tested methods for stress relief. It constantly strives for immediate results and believes in a, more or less, completely stress-free state of every single individual.

Although it promotes and applies universal methods for everyone, Antistress Society also reaches out to specific social groups with appropriate means and in a unique and original way. Among such groups are included:

  • Workforce
  • Entrepreneurs
  • Old-aged
  • Children
  • Women
  • Handicapped
  • Abused
  • Depressed
  • etc.

Strategy

Antistress Society is organized as lean and informal as possible. Its backbone is a relatively small but global network of volunteers, donors and sympathizers. Mutual trust in all matters of the association is the be-all and end-all.

Antistress Society maintains close contact with associations with the same or a similar purpose abroad, especially in India and the USA. Its activities are constantly based on the dynamics of the ever-changing world. In the entire spectrum of its activities, the focus is primarily laid on the efficient and effective use of the internet and IT technologies. Conventional kind of charity work, however, is carried out selectively when the need and circumstances require it.
